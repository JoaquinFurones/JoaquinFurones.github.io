Joaquin Furones

Backend developer in JAVA for a Electric vehicle proyect.
With a side interest in Game development and all aspects related to it, I spend a important part of my free time working on my own proyects like the ones listed below.

Bycicle Simulator

It consist in my End-of-degree work, It is a fully functional program, with a client-server distribution, a conection with an API REST and a MongoDB database, working with a real static bike through SocketIO and BlueTooth LE.
It contains a Login/Register funcionality, a selection menu of diferent levels in a city, User panel and administrator Rol.
Here is my knowledge of some physics for the movement on the bicicle, an AI-powered city, with cars detecting traffic lights, other cars, obstacles, and analyzing priorities at intersections using the Gley system.

Made with Unity

2D Simple plataformer

This is my long term Work-in-progress proyect of developing a dynamic Metroidvania
Using Universal Render Pipeline, 2D-Physics, 2D animations (by now the spear and rope showcased) and general Map creation mecanics like enter buildings, Day-Night cycle, or Parallax.

Made with Unity

AVENTURA'S ADVENTURE

This is a fully finished game, with basic player detection mechanics, different enemy attacks, a fully animated playable character, a boss fight and level design. In this project I tried to reflect knowledge of environment design, in a LowPoly scenario creating the levels from scratch.

Made with Unity

THE SEARCH

This is a project with an outstanding graphic design using a character from Paragon.
It is a third-person shooter, making two levels; one with platforms, and another with enemies, making use of several actionables to progress.
This project tries to reflect the knowledge of the creation of mechanics such as shooting, distance-combat against enemies, basic artificial intelligence of enemies, or platforms, as well as demonstrate the good integration of the mechanics developed with striking graphics and lighting.

Made with Unreal Engine 4

SOME THIRD-PERSON MECHANICS

By this point I wanted to center in developing mecanics, instead of finishing a whole game, especially around the movement of the character, based on mechanics of other games or series that have caught my attention.
In the demo you can see mechanics such as:
- Changing the gravity of the world, in reference to Gravity Rush.
- Rope swinging movement, Spiderman or AOT style.
- Movement of thrown by ropes, Attack on Titan style.
- Continuation of jumping movement.
- Scanning the environment to look for upper corners where you can jump.
- Advanced artificial intelligence, with visual cone detection, sound detection, combat, and patrolling along pre-established paths.
- Stealth mode, noise reduction.
- Combat with on-screen text, and damage scaling for each hit.
- Combos of cancelable blows.
- Block and parry system.
- Target locking system.
- Wall running and hang to ledge.
- Save/Load/Reset game.Assesination and elimination of the corpse by disolving.
- UI Health, Stamina, and XP. With Stamina control to be able hit or block.

Made with Unreal Engine 4
